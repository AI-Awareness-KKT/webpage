About Us

The National Council for Science & Technology Communication (NCSTC), under the Department of Science & Technology (DST), Government of India, collaborates with the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK) to conduct a children-centric outreach program titled “Creating Awareness in Artificial Intelligence.”

Our National Outreach Impact

Through the AI Awareness Project, we have successfully conducted outreach programs across multiple regions in India, directly engaging with school students and educators.

Our initiative has reached many schools and impacted thousands of students, introducing them to Artificial Intelligence through interactive sessions, hands-on experiments, and real-world demonstrations.

The program is currently implemented across Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala, fostering early AI literacy and technological curiosity among young learners.

Click on the map to explore state-wise outreach

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a future where young minds are empowered with the knowledge and skills to thrive in a technology-driven world. We aim to demystify Artificial Intelligence (AI) and make it accessible to students, fostering curiosity, creativity, and innovation in the field of AI.

Our Supporters

Centre for System Design (CSD)

The Centre for System Design (A Centre of Excellence at NITK Surathkal) envisages an interdisciplinary approach and means for realization of successful engineering systems. Key components include system modeling & simulation, understanding system dynamics, system optimization, virtual and physical experimentation. The Centre facilitates an environment for solving modern engineering problems that transcend traditional boundaries, ensuring integration to meet overall design objectives.

Department of Science & Technology (DST)

The Department of Science & Technology (DST) was established in May 1971 with the objective of promoting new areas of science and technology. As a nodal department of the Government of India, DST is responsible for organizing, coordinating, and promoting S&T activities across the country. NCSTS-DST has sponsored this project to ensure the dissemination of AI knowledge and the development of scientific temperament among students.
